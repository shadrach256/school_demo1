How to Apply for Scholarships

Follow these steps to be considered for financial aid at Uganda Institute of Technical Excellence:

  1. Complete the regular admission application through our online portal or in-person at the admissions office
  2. Submit a separate scholarship application form (available from the financial aid office or website)
  3. Provide all required supporting documents including:
    • Academic records and certificates
    • Two recommendation letters
    • Personal statement (for merit-based scholarships)
    • Proof of income (for need-based scholarships)
  4. Ensure all materials are submitted before the deadline
  5. Shortlisted candidates may be invited for an interview
