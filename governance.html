Our Leadership Structure

The Uganda Institute of Technical Excellence operates under a robust governance framework that ensures academic excellence, financial accountability, and institutional development. Our structure aligns with national education policies and international best practices for higher education institutions.

Governing Council

The supreme governing body responsible for policy formulation and strategic direction of the institute. Comprises representatives from government, industry, academia, and alumni.

View Members

Management Board

Oversees the day-to-day administration and implementation of council policies. Chaired by the Institute Director with all department heads as members.

View Members

Academic Board

Ensures maintenance of academic standards and quality assurance across all programs. Includes deans, senior lecturers, and external examiners.
