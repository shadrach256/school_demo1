Accredited Programs

All our engineering programs are fully accredited by the Uganda Engineering Registration Board (UERB) and recognized throughout East Africa.

Our Engineering Facilities

Students benefit from access to state-of-the-art facilities that provide hands-on experience with industry-standard equipment:

  • Fully equipped mechanical workshops with CNC machines
  • Automation and robotics labs with PLC training stations
  • CAD/CAM design studios with latest software
  • Materials testing laboratory with universal testing machines
  • High-voltage electrical engineering lab
  • Hydraulics and pneumatics training systems
  • Civil engineering materials and structures lab
  • Electronics and microcontroller development labs
