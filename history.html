50th Anniversary

50 Years of Technical Excellence

The Uganda Institute of Technical Excellence has a rich history of providing quality technical education since its establishment in 1972. What began as a small technical college has grown into Uganda's premier institution for engineering and technology education.

1972

Institution Founded

Established as Uganda Technical College with three engineering departments and an initial intake of 120 students.

1985

First Expansion

Added new vocational programs and expanded campus facilities to accommodate growing student numbers.

1995

Institute Status Achieved

Granted institute status and renamed to Uganda Institute of Technical Excellence, marking our transition to higher education.

2010

International Accreditation

Received international accreditation for our engineering programs from the Engineering Council of East Africa.

2022

Golden Jubilee Celebration

Celebrated 50 years of excellence in technical education with the opening of our new Innovation and Research Center.
