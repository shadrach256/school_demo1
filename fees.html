Payment Information

All fees are payable in Uganda Shillings (UGX). Fees can be paid through the following methods:

  • Bank transfer: Stanbic Bank, Account Name: Uganda Institute of Technical Excellence, Account Number: 9030001234567
  • Mobile money: MTN Mobile Money (0772123456), Airtel Money (0756123456) - Use student name as reference
  • Cash payments: At the finance office (Monday-Friday, 8:30am-4:00pm)
  • Online payment: Through our secure payment portal (available after registration)

Installment Plans

We offer flexible payment plans for students who need them. The standard installment plan allows for:

  • 50% payment at registration
  • 25% payment by mid-semester
  • 25% payment before final examinations

Special arrangements can be made for students with demonstrated financial need. Contact the finance office at finance@uite.ac.ug or call +256 414 123456 for details.

Financial Aid Options

The Uganda Institute of Technical Excellence offers several financial assistance programs to qualified students:

Merit Scholarships

Available to top-performing students based on academic excellence and leadership potential.

Need-Based Grants

For students demonstrating significant financial need. Requires documentation.

Work-Study Program

Part-time employment opportunities on campus to help offset tuition costs.
