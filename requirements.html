Entry Requirements

Our admission requirements vary by program level and type. Below are the general requirements for our different programs. All applicants must meet both the general institutional requirements and specific program requirements.

Certificate Programs

  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 passes
  • Minimum of a pass in English and Mathematics
  • Completed application form with required documents
  • Copy of birth certificate or national ID
  • Two recent passport-sized photographs

Diploma Programs

  • Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) with 1 principal pass and 2 subsidiaries
  • Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) with at least 5 passes including English
  • Completed application form with certified copies of academic documents
  • Recommendation letter from previous institution
  • Copy of national ID or passport

Vocational Programs

  • UCE with at least 3 passes including English
  • Basic literacy and numeracy skills
  • Completed application form with supporting documents
  • Medical certificate of fitness (for certain trades)
  • Passion for hands-on technical work

Note: Some programs may have additional specific requirements. Please check the individual program pages for details. Mature age entry candidates (25+ years) may qualify through alternative pathways - contact our admissions office for details.

Additional Requirements for All Applicants

  • Non-refundable application fee payment receipt
  • Original academic documents for verification
  • Character reference from a reputable community leader
  • Valid email address and phone number for communication
  • Bank payment details slip for tuition payment
  • International students must provide equivalent qualifications
  • Transfer students must provide transcripts from previous institution
  • Some programs may require an entrance examination or interview
